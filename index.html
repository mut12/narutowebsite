NARUTO UZUMAKI

The hero of the story, Uzumaki Naruto!! I am the Hidden Leaf's Number One Loudest Ninja. I break the rules and I always have to be the centre of attention. My intelligence sinks below every other ninja and I'm kinda untalented in comparison with my fellow Academy students. A lot of people say I'm outspoken and egotistical but what's most importantly to me is that I have a Ninja spirit that never gives up! Sealed within me is a Nine-Tailed Demon Fox that attacked and almost destroyed the Hidden Leaf Village. This immeasurable power was preserved inside of me so that it couldn't return to demolish the Village again.
